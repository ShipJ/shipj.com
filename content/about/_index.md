I'm a Data Scientist and Engineer with a decade of experience building things that actually get used — predictive models, data platforms, analytics infrastructure — mostly in retail and e-commerce.

I was made redundant at the start of 2025 and decided to stop waiting and start building the consulting practice I'd always planned. This site is part of that journey.

#### Professional life

I spent 8 years at [WGSN](https://www.wgsn.com), the world's leading trend authority, most recently leading the Data Science and Engineering teams. My job was to transform a publishing house into a data-driven product company — no small feat. I wrote more about what that actually meant [here]().

Along the way I led teams on projects spanning computer vision, search, fraud detection, churn prediction, time-series forecasting, and large-scale data migrations. The common thread: turning messy, ambiguous data problems into reliable, production-ready systems.

**Leadership**

I align with servant leadership — not because it sounds good, but because it's the fastest way to get a multiplier effect from a team. Every manager I've had told me to choose between the technical and managerial track. I never bought it. The best technical leaders stay hands-on; the best engineering managers understand the code. I've spent 10 years trying to prove both can coexist.
