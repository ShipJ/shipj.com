## Overview

The Acquisition & Sale project focused on enabling a seamless data-driven transition during WGSN’s strategic sale.

I led migrations of key BI and data science tools, supported due diligence with high-quality extracts, and implemented data hygiene processes that enhanced Salesforce and other business-critical datasets. This ensured stakeholders had accurate, reliable, and actionable data throughout the acquisition process.

Core Technologies and Approach

Data Migration & Tool Integration:

Migrated core systems including Jira, Git, Looker, GCP, Databricks, and Marketo to ensure continuity of BI and data operations.

Established Clean Rooms and secure data pipelines to facilitate safe sharing of sensitive information during due diligence.

Oversaw workflows to ensure smooth handoff to acquiring parties.

Due Diligence & Reporting:

Provided structured data extracts and reports to support financial, operational, and technical evaluation.

Coordinated across teams to verify accuracy and completeness, ensuring confidence in reported KPIs.

Data Hygiene & Enrichment:

Led a data cleaning program for Salesforce, enhancing records with business metadata to improve usability and accuracy.

Implemented validation checks and documentation to maintain data quality post-migration.

Impact & Outcomes

Enabled strategic acquisition by ensuring all required data was accurate, accessible, and audit-ready.

Seamless tool migration ensured minimal disruption to ongoing operations.

Enhanced Salesforce data quality, improving long-term usability and reporting.

Built frameworks for data governance during high-stakes business transitions.

Future Expansion

Potential next steps include:

Creating repeatable acquisition-ready pipelines for future M&A activity.

Automating data quality reporting during transition periods.

Integrating business intelligence dashboards specifically for acquisition monitoring.

Documenting best practices for cross-system migrations and clean room compliance.
